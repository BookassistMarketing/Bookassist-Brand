## Fonts

Two fonts are in use.

### Montserrat Extrabold
Used for **page headers and strong banner text**.

- We do not use other weights of Montserrat. Reserved for headers and impact.
- The corporate logo "Bookassist" is a variation of Montserrat Extrabold.
- Download: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Montserrat

### Roboto
Used for all body text.

- **Regular body text:** Roboto Light
- **Headings within text:** Roboto Bold
- **Emphasis:** Roboto Light Italic
- Line spacing: 1 line or 0.9 lines
- Paragraph spacing: 1 line
- Download: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Roboto

## Terminology

Phrases that need continual repetition and re-emphasis.

| Term | Guidance |
|---|---|
| **We make your bookings more profitable** | Repeat on every occasion and at every opportunity. Use at the end of any list of features or descriptions of strategy — e.g. "and that's how we make your bookings more profitable." |
| **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)** | Always refer to the cost per acquisition, not just "the cost", "cost of booking", or "OTA cost". CPA is well defined in hospitality and enables better comparisons between channels. Example: 1000 direct bookings/year at a €5000/year website = €5 CPA. Growing to 2000 bookings drops CPA to €2.50. |
| **Customer Journey** | We base all our technology and strategy on optimising the customer journey — not just for one booking, but to make it *repeatable* and constantly evolving into a Perpetual Cycle of Growth. High ROI at each stage, technology integrated for a smooth journey. It's the holistic Bookassist Ecosystem, not just a group of products. |
| **Perpetual Cycle of Growth** | We constantly refine the Customer Journey to turn it into a Perpetual Cycle of Growth. It's never about just one booking — we show you how to unlock the Customer Journey again and again. |
| **Bookassist Ecosystem** | Similar to Suite but more specific — shows how all products work together and rely on each other to deliver better conversion, higher ROI, and make your bookings more profitable. |
| **Suite, Digital Suite** | Our products work best together as a suite, or Digital Suite, to make your bookings more profitable. Avoid using "group" or other collective terms. |
| **Omni-channel** | Refer to Digital Media as an *omni-channel* promotional service — not multi-channel. Emphasises visibility everywhere at once, including for users, search bots, and AI. |
| **Personalisation** | Continually emphasise personalisation. In Web Design: content can be altered based on geography, browsing history, etc. In Booking Platform: Rate Recommender selects optimum rates based on dates, occupancy, and other metrics. |
| **Next generation, next-gen** | Refer to Web Design (WD) and Booking Platform (BP) as using *next generation* technology — new platforms built on the latest technologies, not upgrades on previous platforms. |
| **Intelligently designed** | Emphasises that the front-end design and UI for Web and Booking Platform is deliberately thought about and is part of the product engineering approach. |
